How Scoring Works
FinBERT Sentiment
Each headline is sent through FinBERT (ProsusAI/finbert).
score = positive − negative
Range: −1.0 (fully negative) to +1.0 (fully positive)
Aggregate Score
Σ(score × relevance × recency × weight) / Σ weights
10-minute sliding window. Older news decays linearly.
Score is clamped to [−1, +1].
Relevance Filter
Headlines are pre-scored by keyword matching before FinBERT.
High relevance: Fed, CPI, interest rates, earnings…
Items below 30% relevance are stored but not analysed.
Direction Thresholds
▲ Bullish: score ≥ +0.35
● Neutral: −0.35 to +0.35
▼ Bearish: score ≤ −0.35
These can be adjusted in score_engine.py
